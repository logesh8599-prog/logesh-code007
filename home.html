Turbo Drive Fest

Turbo Drive Fest is an exciting automotive event that brings together car lovers, speed enthusiasts, and thrill seekers in one place. The festival showcases a stunning collection of supercars, sports cars, and modified vehicles from top brands, offering visitors a chance to experience the power and beauty of high-performance machines. Along with car displays, the event features live stunt shows, racing demonstrations, music, and interactive activities that create an electrifying atmosphere. Whether you are passionate about engines or just enjoy high-speed entertainment, Turbo Drive Fest delivers an unforgettable experience filled with energy, innovation, and adrenaline. One of the main attractions of Turbo Drive Fest is the live action arena, where professional drivers perform breathtaking stunts such as drifting, burnouts, and high-speed racing. These performances create a thrilling environment and allow the audience to witness the true capabilities of these machines. In addition, visitors often get opportunities for test drives and virtual racing experiences, making the event more interactive and engaging. The festival also provides a platform for automobile brands, startups, and car enthusiasts to connect and share ideas. Workshops, expert talks, and networking sessions help visitors learn more about car engineering, maintenance, and customization. Overall, Turbo Drive Fest is a dynamic event that combines entertainment, education, and passion, making it a must-visit for anyone who loves cars and speed.

Lamborghini
•Engine Type:
Most Lamborghini cars use V10 or V12 engines (some new models are hybrid V12 + electric motors).
•Engine Capacity:
Around 5.2L (V10) or 6.5L (V12) engines are common.
•Horsepower (Power)
Typically ranges from 600 HP to 1000+ HP depending on the model.
•Torque:
Around 500 Nm to 1000+ Nm (very high pulling power).
•Top Speed:
Usually 300 km/h to 350+ km/h.
Acceleration (0–100 km/h) Extremely fast — about 2.5 to 3.2 seconds.
BMW - 5 Series

•Engine:

•Power Output:
Produces around 255 HP (horsepower).
•Torque:
Generates about 400 Nm torque for strong performance.
•Transmission:
Equipped with an automatic (8-speed) gearbox.
•Mileage (Fuel Efficiency):
Gives approximately 10–15 km/l depending on driving conditions.

Ferrari

•Engine Type:
Ferrari cars usually have V8 or V12 engines (some newer models are hybrid).
•Engine Capacity:
Typically ranges from 3.9L (V8) to 6.5L (V12).
•Power Output:
Produces around 600 HP to 1000+ HP depending on the model.
•Top Speed:
Can reach about 320 km/h to 340+ km/h.
•Acceleration (0–100 km/h):
Very fast — around 2.5 to 3.0 seconds.
